Formula 1 Visualisation

Formula One is the highest class of international racing for open-wheel single-seater formula racing cars. The World Drivers' Championship has been one of the premier forms of racing around the world since its inaugural season in 1950.

F1 world championship is a formula one racing event that is held annually. The 2022 world championship will be the 73rd edition of the event. The winner of the world championship will be determined by the number of points scored in each race. There are a total of 22 races in the 2022 season, with each race worth a different number of points. The winner of the world championship will be the driver who scores the most points over the course of the season.

Formula 1 Individual driver performance by year

There have been some exceptional Formula 1 drivers over the years, with Lewis Hamilton currently holding the record for the most points in a season (413 in 2019). However, there have been other drivers who have been equally as successful over the years. One of the most successful drivers of all time is Michael Schumacher, who shares the record for the most Formula 1 championships (7) with Lewis Hamilton, Schumacher was dominant during his peak years in the early 2000s, and is widely regarded as one of the greatest drivers of all time. Another driver who has had great success in Formula 1 is Sebastian Vettel. Vettel has won 4 championships, and is currently the second most successful driver of all time. Vettel has been particularly successful in recent years, winning 3 championships in a row between 2010 and 2012. Hamilton is currently the most successful active Formula 1 driver, with 7 championships to his name. And is currently regarded as one of the best drivers in the world.
